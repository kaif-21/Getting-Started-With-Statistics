## 📌 About Statistics

Statistics is a branch of mathematics that focuses on **collecting, organizing, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data**.

It helps us understand patterns in data and make informed decisions based on numerical information.

Statistics is widely used in many fields such as:

* Data Science
* Machine Learning
* Business Analytics
* Economics
* Research
* Artificial Intelligence
